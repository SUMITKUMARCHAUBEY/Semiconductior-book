The Semiconductor in Equillibrium

Equillibrium distribution of electrons and holes:

The distribution of electrons:

n(E) = gc(E) fF(E)

fF(E) is the Fermi Dirac probability function.

g c(E) is the Density of quantum states in the conduction band.

Distribution of holes in valence band:

p(E) = gv(E)[1 - fF(E)]
