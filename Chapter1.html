The Crystal Structure of Solids

Important Formulae

Theoritical Density Calculation

ρ = (n.Aw)/Vcell.Na

  • ρ = Theoritical Density
  • n = Effective Number of Atoms per unit cell
  • Aw = Atomic Weight
  • Vcell = Volume of the Unit cell
  • Na = Avogadro’s Number = 6.022*1023

    • Note: Effective Number of Atoms per unit cell of different Crystal Structure

  •    nSC = 1, nBCC = 2 , nFCC = 4,nHCP = 6, nDC = 8

    • Surface Density

    Surface Density = (Effective number of atoms per lattice plane)/(area of lattice plane)

    Volume Density

    Volume Density = (Effective number of atoms per unit cell)/(volume of unit cell)
