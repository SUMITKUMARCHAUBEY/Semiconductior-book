About Us

This website is a group collaboration project which aims to discuss the formulas of the book Semiconductor Physics and Devices Basic Principles Fourth Edition by Donald A. Neamen. Donald Neamen is a professor and associate chairman for the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of New Mexico.

This project was executed by the students of IIIT SENAPATI, MANIPUR of the batch ECE-2020 in the form of teams of three students. The project idea was assigned to the students by the professor of the course Semiconductor Devices, Dr Gaurav Saxena.
